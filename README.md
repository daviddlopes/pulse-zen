# PulseZen

**PulseZen** is a calm and soothing breathing app designed to help users relax, focus, and reduce stress. With guided breathing cycles, animated visuals, and gentle soundscapes, it provides a simple yet effective way to practice mindfulness anytime, anywhere.

![PulseZen Splash](./assets/splash.png)

---

## Features

- **Guided Breathing Exercises** – Inhale, hold, exhale, hold, repeat.
- **Animated Circular Visuals** – Circle expands and contracts to guide your breathing.
- **Customizable Timings** – Adjust inhale, hold, and exhale durations to fit your needs.
- **Relaxing Backgrounds** – Smooth gradients for a calming visual experience.
- **Lightweight & Fast** – Minimalist design for low battery consumption.
